## Project Overview
This project analyzes a retail business scenario where sales are increasing but profit margins are declining.
The objective is to identify profit leakage, quantify margin erosion, and propose strategies to recover profitability without impacting revenue growth.

---

## Business Problem
Despite strong sales growth, leadership observed declining profits across multiple regions.
The CFO requested a root-cause analysis focusing on:
- Discounts
- Product mix
- Regional performance

---

## Objectives
- Identify profit-draining categories and products
- Analyze discount impact on margin
- Detect regional profitability issues
- Propose pricing and discount guardrails

---

## Dataset
- Sample Superstore Dataset
- Transaction-level retail sales data
- Includes products, categories, regions, discounts, sales, and profit

---

## Tools & Technologies
- SQL
- Python (Pandas)
- Power BI
- Excel (validation)
- GitHub (documentation)

---

## Key Metrics
- Revenue
- Profit
- Profit Margin %
- Average Discount %
- Loss-Making Product Count

---

## Analysis Summary
- Trend analysis of revenue vs profit
- Profitability by category and sub-category
- Discount band impact on margin
- Identification of top loss-making products

---

## Deliverables
- SQL scripts for profitability analysis
- Python notebook for deeper modeling
- Power BI executive dashboard
- Profit recovery recommendations

---

## Outcome
This project provides leadership with a data-driven plan to recover margin while sustaining sales growth.
