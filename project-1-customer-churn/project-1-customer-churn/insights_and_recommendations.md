# Key Insights & Recommendations – Customer Churn Analysis

## Key Insights

1. Early-Life Churn
- Over 70% of churned customers leave within the first 6 months
- Indicates onboarding and early experience issues

2. Contract Type Impact
- Month-to-month customers churn at nearly 3× the rate of annual contract customers
- Long-term contracts significantly improve retention

3. Payment Method Risk
- Customers using electronic check show higher churn
- Suggests payment friction or lower engagement

4. Revenue at Risk
- A small group of high-churn customers contributes disproportionately to monthly revenue loss

---

## Business Recommendations

1. Contract Migration Strategy
- Incentivize month-to-month users to move to annual contracts
- Offer loyalty discounts after 3–6 months

2. Early Customer Retention Program
- Proactive engagement during first 90 days
- Welcome offers, usage education, and support follow-ups

3. Payment Experience Improvement
- Encourage auto-pay and digital wallets
- Improve billing transparency for electronic check users

4. Churn Risk Monitoring
- Create alerts for customers with tenure < 6 months
- Flag high monthly charge customers for retention outreach

---

## Expected Impact
- 5–8% churn reduction
- Improved customer lifetime value
- Reduced revenue leakage
